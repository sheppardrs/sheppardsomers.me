How Sharity Works!

Overview

You can get involved in the community by completing a profile outlining some of the highlights on your path through life, some of your most important values, or you most meaningful goals. After sharing generally who you are as a person on your profile, you will be able to post an ask, tag it with different causes or pieces about you, and explain how some item will make a difference to you. Sharity provides a platform to connect people around used goods. Take your old items and let them make a difference to someone. We'll ask for a few tags around situation so that we can better present your story to the community.

Looking for something:

Post an ask for something you or your loved ones are looking for. It could be anything from a winter coat to a new phone. Tell people what your story is, why you need something, and how it will make a difference to you. Want to learn more about how best to ask? See all the research on the topic: Best Asking Practices

Giving something:

Have something you don't need anymore? Find it a new home. Find a story that resonates with you; make a difference for someone in the community.
